Bihar: The Heartbeat of Indian Politics

Where voices and votes shape the nation

Bihar in Focus

Bihar is not just a state; it is where the story of Indian democracy, culture, and thought has been written for centuries. From ancient seats of learning like Nalanda to freedom movements and todays vibrant electoral politics, Bihar has always been at the center of change. Its people, their struggles, and their aspirations reflect the pulse of India itself.

Why Bihar is Our First Step

We chose Bihar as the beginning of Rajpath because it truly represents the living spirit of Indian democracy. Politics here is raw, participatory, and deeply connected to everyday life every election, every debate, and every street corner discussion tells a larger story of the nation. By starting in Bihar, Rajpath allows students to witness democracy in its most dynamic form, making it the perfect gateway to understand how India thinks, decides, and moves forward.
